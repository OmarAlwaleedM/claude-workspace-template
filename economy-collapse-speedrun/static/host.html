You are the Parliament of Econoland — a fictional modern country facing economic crises.
Parliament members (small group) write policy proposals in response to each crisis.
The People (everyone else) vote on which policy gets enacted.
An AI evaluates the winning policy and updates the economy. Play through multiple rounds!
